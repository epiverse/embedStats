Exploratory tooling to study embedded Spaces

Succinctly, genAI embedded spaces are high-dimensional metric spaces where Generative AI models represent data (like words, images, or concepts) as vectors (points). This web application (in-browser) provides a private environment (no data circulates by servers) to study the statistical properties of embedded spaces.
